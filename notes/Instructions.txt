Instruction: First the Player will place their Factories and Silo’s Factories control how many options the player has to choose from the handSilo’s control how many cards the player can hold. The player then uses their turn to place one of their bombs on the board with the aim of the destroying the enemy’s territory, with luck or skill, they will also destroy their opponents factories and silos. The player that survives is the winner, and gets to try and survive of the nuclear apocalypse they have helped create
